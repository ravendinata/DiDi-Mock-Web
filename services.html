Our Services

DiDi expanded its wings into the Southeast Asian market in 2022 by taking 2 of its most well-known services to the region: Ride-hailing and Car Rental.


Back in China where DiDi was founded, DiDi operates multiple layers of transportation services from basic Ride-hailing, Bike Sharing, Freight, and even Luxury Car Rentals.

We understand that in many Southeast Asian countries many people are still relying on their personal vehicle to commute and travel. So now we are taking our beloved Ride-hailing and Car Rental service to the Southeast Asian market.

Ride Hailing

DiDi's Ride-hailing Service is one of the best in the world. We employ gold standard in our service guidelines to maximize rider safety and convenience when using our services. Our drivers are carefully selected and filterd so as to ensure that all of our drivers are the best drivers that the world could have. Our drivers are also very well trained to always be ready for all situations and conditions. They are required to take defensive driving course, simple repairs course, navigation course, and even beginner martial arts lessons! We want our drivers to be resilient and well-prepared to ensure that you are safe and sound when travelling with DiDi.

DiDi Ride Hailing is fully available in Singapore and Malaysia*.

*) Please check our regions page to see if DiDi Ride Hailing is available in your area or not.

Car Rental

DiDi's Car Rental service is designed to provide you with the best car rental service in the Southeast Asian market. We have a large fleet of cars that are well-maintained and ready for use. All cars that are available are fully insured and have been inspected by our team of professional car inspectors. You don't have to worry about any of the safety issues that you may face when using our car rental service. Cars for rent are always checked for safety every month to ensure that you have the best experience when using our car rental service.

Benefits of renting from DiDi
  1. Free insurance. Rent a car worry-free with our free insurance included in all rental orders.
  2. No hidden fees. No hidden fees when you rent a car from DiDi.
  3. Free road side assistance. Flat tire? Battery died? Car broke down? Give us a call and our team will help you anywhere you are within covered regions.
  4. Free car change. Whenever you call our team due to car issues, our team will take an extra for you to use. Our time is precious. So do yours! We don't want to keep you stranded on the roadside!

DiDi Car Rental is fully available in Singapore.
