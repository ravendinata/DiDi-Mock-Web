What is DiDi?

DiDi is a Chinese company that provides app-based transportation services, including taxi hailing, private car hailing, social ride-sharing, and bike sharing; on-demand delivery services; and automobile services, including sales, leasing, financing, maintenance, fleet operation, electric vehicle charging, and co-development of vehicles with automakers.

DiDi is not just a ride-hailing company.
DiDi is a leading company for all things vehicle.
